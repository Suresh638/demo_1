Overview

✓Arasan Eye Hospital was established in Erode in the year 1997.
It had a modest beginning in rented premises.


✓ But from the beginning, the aim was to cater to all segments of society.
Every week, free eye camps were conducted for the benefit of the rural community.


✓ Arasan is a full-service tertiary eye care centre with specialties
like cataract, glaucoma, retina, paediatric ophthalmology, orbit, and oculoplasty.
